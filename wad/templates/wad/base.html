About

A tool for contractors and freelancers who need to stay under a contractual working-day limit. If your contract caps your working days per year, this helps you figure out how many days off you still need to book.

Set your home and client countries, and public holidays for both are loaded automatically. Overlapping holidays - days that are public holidays in both countries - are highlighted so you can prioritise booking those first.

The dashboard tracks your days-off budget: the number of weekdays you need to take off to stay within your limit. Mark days off on the calendar and watch your budget update in real time.
